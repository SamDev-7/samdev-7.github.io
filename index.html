About Me

Hello, my name is Sam. I'm a student who is passionate about programming. I started my programming journey in grade 4 when my teacher introduced me to Hour of Code. At the time, I was fascinated by how simple it was and the possibilities of code. Scratch was my first big platform for programming. Block coding was easy to work with.

I later switched to text-based coding, as Scratch was quite limited with what it could do. And as a beginner, I started with Python. I first created some console programs, mostly simple calculators and storytelling. I then discovered Discord; Its bot API sparked my interest. Discord bot development allowed me to further enhance my knowledge. It allowed me to understand best practices when writing code, and my code got better.

Later, I moved on to web development, as it was something anyone could see. At first, CSS was really hard, but with the help of Tailwind CSS, I successfully made some simple sites. I then had to learn JavaScript. After some struggles, I was able to use simple APIs like Open-Meteo to integrate weather data into my site. I then learned more complex web development. I used Python, and although it's not the best language for this use case, it was the most comfortable language for me. I used Flask to create dynamic sites, and I learned how to use databases such as MongoDB.

And now, here I am, working on my own personal website. My own personal website allows me to share what I do and what I'm passionate about. Scroll down further to learn about my projects.
